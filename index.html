Our Story

We Don't Just Build Homes.
We Build Relationships.

Paynter Construction is a family-owned small business run by Chip — a firefighter, veteran, and craftsman with over 30 years of construction experience. We're a self-made company from a small town, and we treat every project like it's our own backyard.

We specialize in ADUs, decks, sheds, and awnings across Loomis, Rocklin, Roseville, and the surrounding foothills. From the first call to the final board, our goal is simple: make you feel comfortable, keep you informed, and build something you're proud to show off.
