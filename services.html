Custom Awnings

We specialize in custom wood awnings that protect your home and outdoor spaces year-round. Built on-site or fabricated off-site and delivered — your choice.

The right awning does double duty: it keeps the brutal summer sun from blasting through your windows, and shields your deck, patio, or outdoor furniture from the harsh winter weather for many years to come.

Reduces interior cooling costs in the summer
Extends usable outdoor living space into the rain season
Protects doors, windows, and siding from UV and moisture
