Service 02

Custom Awnings

We specialize in custom wood awnings — built on-site from timber or fabricated off-site and delivered. The right awning does double duty: it keeps the brutal summer sun from blasting through your windows, and shields your deck, patio, and outdoor furniture from the harsh winter weather for years to come.

Every awning is designed to match your home's style and property layout. No two are identical.

Reduces interior cooling costs in summer
Extends outdoor living space year-round
Protects doors, windows & siding from UV and moisture
Built on-site or delivered — your preference

ADUs & Tiny Homes

Paynter Construction is a Tiny Home Preferred Contractor. We've built ADUs across the foothills — from aging parent suites to rental units to young family spaces — and we manage everything from site work to permits to final walkthrough.

Your ADU can be custom-built on your property or delivered via trailer. No matter the need, we design with a sense of home. Every detail planned with comfort and livability in mind.
